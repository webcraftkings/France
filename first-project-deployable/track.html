Track Your Application Status

Your visa application is monitored by the visa centre where you submitted it. You may check the progress of your application, but:

Note: No information will be given as to whether your visa will be issued or not.

Where Did You Submit Your Application?

You can monitor your visa application status anytime via your "My Applications" area on France-Visas.

If you applied through a CAPAGO visa application center:

  • Use the CAPAGO online tracking tool
  • Provide the following to access your status:
    • Reference of your file
    • Your email address

Visa Application Center Guidance

TLS Contact Applicants

You submitted your visa application at a TLS visa center. You can:

  • Track your application via the TLS online tool
  • Log into your TLS account to monitor your status

VFS Global Applicants

If you applied through a VFS visa center:

  • Use the VFS online tracking portal
  • You will need:
    • Your reference number
    • Your last name

Helpful Info

To find specific instructions related to your location:

  • Visit the local visa center page
  • Check the “Whom should I contact?” section for assistance

Passport Collection

When your visa application has been processed, you will be notified to collect your passport from the center you visited.
