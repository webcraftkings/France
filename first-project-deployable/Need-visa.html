Do You Need a Visa?

Fill out the form below to determine your visa requirements.

As of April 2025, U.S. citizens are required to obtain a visa prior to entering France. Travelers who arrive without a valid visa may be denied entry.

Important Information

Please ensure that you have all the necessary documents ready before filling out this form.

  • Passport with at least 6 months validity.
  • Recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Proof of accommodation in France.
  • Travel itinerary.
  • Financial proof for the duration of your stay.
