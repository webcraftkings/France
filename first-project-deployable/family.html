Family Purpose

If you want to visit your family without settling permanently, refer to the tourist and private stay section for conditions.

If you're planning to join a family member who lives or has settled in France, the documents and type of visa you need will depend on:

  • Whether your relative is an EU/EEA national
  • Whether your relative is French
  • Whether your relative is a foreign national residing in France

If you live in France and wish to adopt a minor from abroad, consult the conditions for an adoption visa.
